Why Backlinks Are the Backbone of SEO

Backlinks serve as "votes of confidence" from one website to another. Search engines like Google interpret these links as a signal that your content is valuable, credible, and useful. However, not all links are created equal.

Dofollow vs. Nofollow

When engaging in backlink activity, understanding the difference is key:

  • Nofollow links: These tell search engines not to pass authority to the linked site. They generate traffic but minimal SEO juice.
  • Dofollow backlinks: These are the gold standard. A dofollow backlink allows search engine crawlers to follow the link to your site, passing authority and directly improving your rankings.

The Power of Social Bookmarking

Social bookmarking is a method for internet users to store, organize, search, and manage bookmarks of web pages on the internet. For SEOs, it is a legitimate strategy to gain quick indexing and valuable link juice.

Unlike complex guest posting strategies that take weeks, social bookmarking offers a faster route to build a diverse link profile.
